Weapon Definitions and Impact

Assault Rifles: Balanced firepower and accuracy, ideal for medium-range combat.

Submachine Guns (SMGs): Fast fire rate with lower damage, great for close-quarters fights.

Sniper Rifles: High precision and one-shot kill potential at long range.

Shotguns: Devastating at close range, but ineffective at long distances.

Light Machine Guns (LMGs): Large magazine capacity with moderate recoil, suitable for sustained fire.

Pistols: Sidearms with quick draw speeds but limited damage.
